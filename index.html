🏗️ Conference Theme 2026

Rebuilding the Temple

Vision 2032 — Raising a Jesus Generation

⛏️

"Rebuilding the Temple – Vision 2032 is not just a theme. It is a move of God within our Kids Ministry."

This vision is about re‑establishing a dynamic, Spirit‑filled Sunday School over the next five to seven years. A place where children do not just attend church, but encounter Jesus, grow in identity, and learn how to live out their faith boldly.

When we speak about rebuilding the temple, we are not only talking about bricks, classrooms, or structures. We are talking about people. More specifically, we are talking about our children.

In Scripture, the temple was the dwelling place of God. In this season, God is reminding us that His Spirit dwells in people. Our kids are His living temples. As we rebuild, we are allowing God to restore foundations, reshape vision, and establish something that will last for generations.

We believe God is calling us to raise Kingdom Builders. Children who will not only follow Jesus, but reflect His heart. Kids who are dynamic, faith‑filled, and unashamed of the Gospel. A true Jesus generation.
